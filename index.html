“Who are you, when nobody is looking?”

It is a question I’ve been asking myself often over the past year at university. As a student studying CS at NYU, all I’ve had to guide me through my professional career is the desire to create– designing and creating things, whether it be videos, programs, or whatever my mind thinks to do is what gives me the most fulfillment.

I've always been a creative person, taking pictures of my family and the sights whilst on vacation, drawing amateurish sketches of things I thought to be beautiful, or making random videos of funny or cool ideas.

Though I may not know where I’m headed exactly, I carry with me the confidence and posture to succeed in whatever field I’m in, whether it be product-design, software development, or entrepreneurial endeavors.
