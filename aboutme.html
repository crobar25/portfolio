about me. who even is this guy

Hi! I'm Connor, and am a student at new york university studying computer science.

During my 4 years at NYU, I plan to make the most out of my time here. That means: excelling in schoolwork, meeting and talking to cracked developers, making projects with founders and creators, and creating art through photography and videography. I aim to become a technically and aesthetically driven developer, and to make applications that solve problems that people are actively facing.

I haven't found out where exactly I want to contribute to in the field of computer science, but I've nonetheless found the journey to be difficult yet rewarding. From learning to sort and clean datasets to building this website, I've learned about so many different applications of algorithms and computer programs, inspiring me to embark on my own projects and programming endeavors.

Feel free to contact me to talk about anything cs or photo/film related! I'd love to meet and collaborate with new people!
